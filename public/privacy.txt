FAMILY GRAPH - PRIVACY POLICY (BETA)
Last Updated: May 29, 2026

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
- Account info: Email, name, phone (if provided)
- Family data: Names, birth years, locations, photos, stories you upload
- Usage data: Pages visited, features used (via Vercel Analytics)
- Device data: Browser type, IP address (for security)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
- To provide the family tree service
- To send you important service updates
- To improve our product (anonymous analytics)
- To provide AI chat features (Google Gemini)

3. DATA SHARING
We share data with:
- Supabase (database hosting) - USA
- Google AI (chat features) - USA  
- Vercel (hosting & analytics) - USA
- Family members you invite to your tree

We DO NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Share publicly without your permission

4. DATA SECURITY
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Row-level security on database
- Regular security updates
- Access controls (only you and invited family see your tree)

5. YOUR RIGHTS
You can:
- Export your data (contact us)
- Delete your account (Settings → Delete Account)
- Control who sees your profile (Privacy settings)
- Revoke family member access (Admin controls)

6. COOKIES
We use minimal cookies for:
- Authentication (required)
- Analytics (Vercel Analytics)
- Theme preference

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
You can add children to your family tree, but they cannot create accounts under age 13.

8. INTERNATIONAL USERS
Data stored in USA. By using our service, you consent to international data transfer.
